## 6. Ads Strategy

### Best Platforms:

**Meta (Facebook/Instagram) — #1**
- CPC: $0.30–$4.00
- CPL avg ~$22 (can hit $10-20 with good targeting)
- Format: Single product ad → one-page sales site
- Target: productivity, AI tools, automation, solopreneur, small business owner | Age 25-55
- Min budget: $10/day to test, $30-50/day for real data
- Retargeting: pixel visitors, show testimonials + urgency

**TikTok — #2**
- Best for: younger solopreneurs / side-hustlers
- Short "day in the life with AI assistant" screen recordings
- CPC roughly $0.20-0.80, lower than Meta
- AI/productivity content performs well
- Min $20/day

**Google Search**
- Capture: "OpenClaw guide," "AI personal assistant setup," "automation guide"
- Higher intent = better conversion, smaller volume
- CPC $3-8

**Reddit**
- Communities: r/productivity, r/ChatGPT, r/entrepreneur, r/sideproject
- CPL $15-40 but warm audiences who already care
- Min $50/day — needs more data to optimize

**X/Twitter**
- Quick tips, screenshots, "AI employee of the day" posts
- Promoted tweets work for $5-10/day
- Build audience before/during launch

### Funnel Structure:
Cold Ad → One-Page Sales Site → Checkout → Thank You
                  ↓
           Email Capture (free tip sheet)
                  ↓
           Retargeting sequence

### One-Page Sales Site Needs:
1. Bold outcome headline
2. 2-3 min screen recording video (VSL)
3. What you get breakdown
4. Before/after comparison
5. Testimonials — CRITICAL
6. Early-bird pricing + countdown
7. 30-day money-back guarantee
8. FAQ block (handles objections)

### Budget Estimates:
For meaningful multi-platform tests:
- Meta: $500-1000/month
- TikTok: $300-500/month
- Google: $200-400/month
- Reddit: $300-500/month
- **Total: ~$1,500-2,500/month**

Start with Meta only if budget is tight. Scale to TikTok once you find what hooks.
