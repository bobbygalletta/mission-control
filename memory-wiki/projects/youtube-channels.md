## Growth Strategy

### Short Term
- Consistent posting schedule (2-3x per week per channel)
- Focus on one channel at a time when burned out
- Engage with comments
- Use shorts for growth

### Long Term
- Build loyal audience
- Diversify content across both channels
- Use channels to promote info products
- Build email list from viewers
- Create sponsored content opportunities
