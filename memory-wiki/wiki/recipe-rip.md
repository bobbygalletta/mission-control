# Recipe Rip

**App idea: Extract recipes from websites automatically.**

---

## Overview
Recipe Rip is a planned app that takes a URL (from a recipe website) and extracts the recipe — ingredients, instructions, cook times — in a clean format. Strip out the ads, life story, and fluff. Just the recipe.

---

## Purpose
- **Solve a real problem** — Recipe sites are cluttered with ads and stories
- **Product potential** — Could be an app or web tool to sell
- **Content utility** — Useful for Bobby's cooking/content work

---

## Status
- **Phase:** On the project list
- **Priority:** Medium (after OpenClaw Guide)
- **Type:** App (mobile or web — not decided)

---

## Core Feature Idea
```
Input:  Recipe blog URL
Output: Clean, formatted recipe
        - Ingredients list
        - Instructions
        - Cook/prep time
        - Servings
        - No ads or fluff
```
