## What Is The Orb?

The "Orb" is a conceptual visual map showing how all files in Bobby's Memory Wiki connect to each other. It's designed to show at a glance how knowledge flows between different areas of Bobby's life.

## Connection Types

### Strong Connections (Solid Lines)
Direct cause-effect or ownership:
- Bobby → Logan (relationship)
- Bobby → OpenClaw Setup (owns/operates)
- Bobby → Income Sources (generates)
- Income Sources → Bills (pays)

### Moderate Connections (Dashed Lines)
Indirect or influence:
- OpenClaw Setup → OpenClaw Guide (source material)
- YouTube Channels → Social Media Strategy (promotion)
- Recipe Rip → Social Media Strategy (promotion)

### Weak Connections (Dotted Lines)
Occasional or tangential:
- Logan → Income Sources (household context)
- Morning Brief → YouTube Channels (occasional mention)

## The Orb Metaphor

Think of each file as a **node** floating in space. The connections between them are like gravitational pulls — some nodes are closely orbit each other (strongly connected), while others are more distant.

**Bobby** is the sun at the center. Everything orbits around him.

**Money** is the inner orbit — Income Sources, Bills, Products.

**Content** is the outer orbit — YouTube, Social Media, Promotion.
