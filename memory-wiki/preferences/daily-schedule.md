## Context Switch Costs

Bobby does many different types of work:
- **Driving/Delivery** (DoorDash)
- **Video creation** (YouTube)
- **Social media** (X, TikTok)
- **Writing** (scripts, guides)
- **Admin** (emails, bills, planning)

Context switching between these is costly. Batching similar tasks helps.

---

## Optimization Opportunities

- Batch all DoorDash shifts together when possible
- Record YouTube videos in batches (3-4 at once)
- Use AI agents to handle routine communications
- Pre-schedule social media posts
