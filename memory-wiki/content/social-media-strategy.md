## Tips for Better Social Media

1. **Batch content creation** — Create multiple posts/videos at once
2. **Repurpose everything** — One YouTube video = 10 X posts = 5 TikToks
3. **Engage authentically** — Real engagement > bot interaction
4. **Track what works** — Use analytics to guide strategy
5. **Consistency beats virality** — Post regularly, not just when inspired
